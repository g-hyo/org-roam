#+title: hypermedia

Hypermedia is an extension of the term [[id:ea80efa2-411f-4c41-9371-2fd0f4e2c22e][hypertext]], to include graphics, audio and video in addition to plain text and hyperlinks.

The World Wide Web is a classic example of hypermedia.
