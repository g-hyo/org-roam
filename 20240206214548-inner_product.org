#+title: inner product

In linear algebra, the inner product also known as a dot product, is an operation defined for vectors that yields a scalar quantity.

For two vectors, v and w, the inner product is calculated as:

\[
v \cdot w = v_{1}w_{1} + v_{2}w_{2} + ... + v_{n}w_{n}
\]
